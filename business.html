What I did there...

I worked with iPhone and iPad versions of Code:Red's app, Code Red RMS on the iTunes App Store. Specifically, I helped fix bugs that were found, and also warnings and errors in the Xcode project. I also helped create the rich text editor module in the app; it was one of my main projects.
